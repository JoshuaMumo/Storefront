About Django

Django is a high-level Python web framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic design. Created by experienced developers, it takes care of much of the hassle of web development, allowing you to focus on writing your app without needing to reinvent the wheel. Django is known for its "batteries-included" philosophy, providing a wide array of built-in features such as an ORM (Object-Relational Mapper), robust authentication system, flexible admin interface, and comprehensive security mechanisms. Its scalability, versatility, and the strong support of a vibrant community make Django a popular choice for building powerful and reliable web applications.
